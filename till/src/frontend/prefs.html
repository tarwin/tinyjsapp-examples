Idle Notifications

When a timer is running and you step away, Till stops it and rolls the idle stretch back out — then sends a notification so you know. Manage how those banners appear in System Settings.

Clock Info
✓ Your system clock is the source of truth

Till relies on your system clock to track time accurately. Everything is computed from it on this Mac — no server, no sync, nothing leaves the machine.
